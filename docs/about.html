SideSpins is a lightweight lineup and availability tool built for APA teams. It helps captains plan match nights, stay under the 23-point skill rule, and coordinate availability without endless text chains.

Who it's for

  • APA team captains managing weekly lineups
  • Teams coordinating player availability
  • Captains who want a better alternative to spreadsheets and group texts

What it does

  • Track player availability by match date
  • View lineup combinations under the skill-point cap
  • Keep lineups private to your team
  • Mobile-friendly interface
  • Passwordless login via SMS

Access model

SideSpins is shared on an invite and request basis. There's no public self-service signup. This keeps team data clean and private while allowing captains to bring their teams on board when they're ready.

If you're an APA captain and this sounds useful, you can request access for your team.
